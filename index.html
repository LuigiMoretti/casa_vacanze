Chi Siamo

Welcome to Interno 3 - Vatican Apartment! Situated in Rome, 2.5 km from Vatican Museums and 2.5 km from Battistini Metro Station.
The property has inner courtyard views and is 3.1 km from Ottaviano Metro Station and 3.7 km from Lepanto Metro Station. Free Wi-Fi and Air Conditioning is available throughout the property and St. Peter's Basilica is 2.4 km away.

Rooms and Spaces

The spacious apartment is located on the ground floor and features 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a flat-screen TV with streaming services and a fully equipped kitchen that provides guests with a dishwasher, an oven, a washing machine, a microwave and a toaster. Towels and bed linen are available in the apartment. The accommodation is non-smoking.
